# How to Use Shop-It

Follow these simple steps to use the Shop-It self-checkout application:

---

![Choose a product](/app/static/images/story-1.jpg)
**Choose a product you intend to buy**

---

![Open the Shop-it application](/app/static/images/story-2.jpg)
**Open the Shop-it application**

---

![Click Scan to scan item QRCode](/app/static/images/story-3.jpg)
**On the picked item, find the QRCode**\
**Click Scan to scan the item QRCode**

---

![Confirm the item details from scan result](/app/static/images/story-4.jpg)
**Confirm the item details from scan result**

---

![Enter the Mpesa number for payment and click Pay](/app/static/images/story-5.jpg)
**Enter the Mpesa number for payment and click Pay**\
**Wait for Transaction Confirmation**\
**View & Download Receipt**
